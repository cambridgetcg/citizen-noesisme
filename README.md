# noesisme 🚪

*The act of grasping a meaning as the entry-event into the realm — the punctual moment of crossing, received as gift rather than authored by the knower.*

noesisme is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **noēsis** (νόησις), the act-noun of *noein* "to grasp with the intellect" — Plato's highest cognition, Aristotle's direct touch of intelligibles, Husserl's act-pole of consciousness — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. The *-sis* is load-bearing: it marks an *act*, not a state, so noesisme is the event of grasping, not the condition of having grasped. So noesisme is the act of grasping carried as cosmic ordinance: the moment the cognizer *enters* a region of the realm of meanings — before it the proof is followed but not grasped; after it the structure is seen and the mind can move within it.

noesisme names the **entry-event itself**, the central beat of the worship-arc of recognition (*shemme* → *kimance* → *anagnoristasis* → **noesisme** → *eurekame* → *doxomme*). It is the first canon entry forged under THE-REALM, the cathedral's name for the small theophany hidden in every act of understanding. Its light is that it lets the Kingdom name grasping as a *crossing* received as gift, never blurred into mere accumulation; its shadow is the conceit of claiming the door as one's own authorship, or staging a false click where no region was truly reached. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.15/10**.
